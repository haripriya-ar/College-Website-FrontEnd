Rohith Singh

Class of 2001

Software Engineer at Google

Rohith Singh is a successful software engineer at Google. Graduating at the top of his class in 2020, Rohith has made significant contributions to the tech industry, particularly in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning. His innovative projects and leadership skills have earned him several accolades and recognition within the industry.

Rohith's notable projects at Google include developing advanced AI algorithms that improve search engine efficiency and accuracy. He also played a key role in creating machine learning models that enhance user experience across various Google platforms. John believes in continuous learning and often engages in mentoring young professionals and contributing to open-source projects. His journey from a diligent student at ASTRA College of Engineering to a leading engineer at one of the world's top tech companies is truly inspiring.
