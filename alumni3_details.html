Dr. Ranjan R Pai

Class of 2018

Product Manager at Microsoft

Dr. Ranjan R Pai is a distinguished product manager at Microsoft. Graduating in 2018, Robert quickly advanced in his career by showcasing his exceptional skills in project management and product development. He has been instrumental in launching several successful software products that have significantly contributed to Microsoft's portfolio.

Ranjan's role involves coordinating cross-functional teams to ensure seamless product delivery and continuous improvement. His strategic vision and ability to navigate complex challenges have earned him a reputation as a reliable and innovative leader. Ranjan actively mentors young professionals and is committed to fostering a culture of innovation and excellence within his team. His journey from a dedicated student at ASTRA College of Engineering to a key player at Microsoft exemplifies his dedication and talent.
