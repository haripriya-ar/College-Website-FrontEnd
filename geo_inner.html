Mtech in Geoinformatics

Course Overview

An M.Tech in Geoinformatics focuses on utilizing advanced technology to analyze, manage, and visualize spatial data for applications in fields such as urban planning, environmental monitoring, and natural resource management.


Specialized training in geographic information systems (GIS) for data analysis and visualization.


Integration of geoinformatics with fields like urban planning, agriculture, disaster management, and transportation.
