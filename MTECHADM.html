Refund Policy

  1. One-time
    • Application fee and Admission Fee are non-refundable.
  2. Application fee and Admission Fee are non-refundable.
    • If a student accepts the admission offer and completes the admission formalities but withdraws before the date of registration, 80% of the first installment of tuition fee is refundable.
    • If a student withdraws within one week of the date of registration irrespective of whether the student registers or not, 50% of the first installment of first semester tuition fee is refundable.
    • If a student withdraws after one week of the date of registration irrespective of whether the student registers or not, the first installment of first semester tuition fee is non- refundable.

Eligibility criteria for M.Tech

  • The following eligibility criteria is applicable for admission to ACE. A candidate seeking admission to a particular programme has to meet the eligibility criteria for that programme as given below:

  • A minimum aggregate of 60% in the qualifying degree as indicated below for each programme.

  • M.E. Design: B.E./B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering or its equivalent.
  • M.E. Electrical (Power Electronics & Drive): B.E./B.Tech. in EEE, E&I or ECE or its equivalent.
  • M.E. Microelectronics: B.E./B.Tech. in EEE/E&I/ECE/CS or M.Sc. Physics or its equivalent.
  • M.E. Software Systems: B.E./B.Tech./M.Sc./M.C.A or equivalent.
  • M.E. Civil (Infrastructure Engg. & Mgmt.) : B.E./B.Tech. in Civil Engineering or its equivalent. (B.E. Mechanical & Electrical may also be considered with some deficiency courses)

  • Candidates who meet the above criteria will be required to appear and successfully clear the Aptitude test/Interview to qualify for admission.
